Motivation:
* Q4: Purpose of the creation of dataset?
Q4-Ans: To provide a structured property-graph representation of scholarly communications for analyzing citations, authorship, keywords, and venues.

* Q5: Which task was intended to solve with this dataset, i.e., data mining, Q/A, entity linking, path query etc?

* Q6: Has this dataset been used before? If yes? Why this dataset was chosen?
Q6-Ans: N/A

* Q7: What is the reason behind the modeling of the graph/dataset?
Q7-Ans: Scholarly data is inherently relational (papers cite papers; authors write papers; papers have keywords and venues), making a property graph suitable for multi-relational queries and analytics.
